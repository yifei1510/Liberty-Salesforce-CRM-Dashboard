# Liberty Australia Lead Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates a Salesforce CRM reporting workflow built for Liberty Exports Australia's Australia market lead management. The goal was to convert raw buyer and supplier lead research into a structured CRM dashboard that supports lead visibility, prioritisation, and follow-up planning.

The final dashboard summarises buyer and supplier leads in Salesforce, helping management quickly understand lead volume, buyer interest, supplier priority, MOQ status, and follow-up readiness.

## Business Objective

The project was designed to support revenue operations and GTM planning by answering three practical business questions:

- How many Australia market leads are currently available in Salesforce?
- Which buyer and supplier leads should be prioritised?
- Which supplier leads require follow-up before outreach or collaboration decisions?

## Dashboard Summary

The Salesforce dashboard includes the following components:

| Dashboard Component | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Total Lead Count | Shows the combined buyer and supplier lead volume |
| Buyer Importance Report | Segments buyer leads by importance level |
| Supplier Follow-up | Tracks supplier leads requiring follow-up confirmation |
| Supplier Priority | Breaks supplier leads into High, Medium to High, and Medium priority |
| Supplier MOQ | Shows supplier MOQ status, including quote-based and negotiation-based opportunities |

## Tools Used

- Salesforce CRM
- Salesforce Leads
- Salesforce Campaigns
- Salesforce Reports
- Salesforce Dashboards
- Excel data cleaning and field mapping

## Workflow

1. Cleaned and structured buyer and supplier lead research data.
2. Mapped spreadsheet fields into Salesforce Lead fields.
3. Imported buyer and supplier leads into Salesforce.
4. Created Salesforce Campaigns to organise lead groups.
5. Built Salesforce reports for buyer importance, supplier priority, MOQ, and follow-up status.
6. Combined the reports into a management-ready Salesforce dashboard.

## Business Value

This dashboard improves CRM visibility by turning raw market research into a structured reporting workflow. It helps the business prioritise high-value suppliers, understand MOQ readiness, and identify leads that require follow-up before outreach.

The project reflects practical revenue operations work, including CRM data hygiene, lead segmentation, dashboard reporting, and GTM decision support.

## Notes

The dashboard was created in a Salesforce development environment for portfolio and internship project demonstration purposes. Sensitive contact details and internal CRM records are not included in this repository.
